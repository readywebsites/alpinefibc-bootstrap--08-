Baffle FIBC

Baffle bag gives improved stability and improved loading efficiency of containers and trucks.

Q-Bag remains within its pallet dimensions when filled. The unique design of the baffles in the bag construction prevents the bag from bulging or falling over.

Our design incorporates baffles across all four corners of the bag. Within these baffles are holes cut out which allow the product to flow evenly throughout the bag. Rather than gluing the baffles, we sew them into the side panels of the FIBC with filler cord. This makes the FIBC sift-proof and capable of storing very fine powders without leaks when stacked or transported.

Options available depending on materials, transportation, Safe Working Load or Safety Factor:
  • • Coated or Uncoated fabric
  • • Safety factor of either 5:1 (single trip) or 6:1 (multi trip)
  • • UN-approved with a safety factor of 6:1
  • • Printed in multicolour on four sides
  • • Variety of document pouches and labels
  • • Dust proof
  • • Various inlet and outlet closures
Additional features depending on filling and discharge system:
  • • Skirt or Duffle Top
  • • Inlet filling Spout
  • • Discharge Spout
  • • Conical Discharge Spout
  • • PE Baffle liner
