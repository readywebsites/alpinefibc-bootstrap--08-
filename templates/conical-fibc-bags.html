Conical FIBC

SEND INQUIRY

FIBCs (big bags/bulk bags) with a conical base are specially developed for poorly flowing products. The conical shape at the bottom of the bag ensures that the bulk bag can be discharged quickly and easily. By using this bulk bags, you can sometimes prevent expensive adjustments to discharging systems.

Specifications

  • SWL Range: 500 kg to 2000 kg.
  • Flat woven or tubular woven fabric
  • Coated or uncoated
  • With or without liner
  • Various lifting possibilities
  • Various discharging possibilities
