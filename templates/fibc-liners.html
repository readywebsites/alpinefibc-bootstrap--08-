Liners for FIBC

Loose, Glued, Tabbed, Stitched Liners, EVOH Liner, Baffle Liner, Aluminum, Foil liner, Conductive, Antistatic liner

A bulk bag can be fitted with an inner bag, which we call a liner. This liner provides additional protective properties against air and water vapor migration and the effect of light.

SHRI KHEMISATI can manufacture liners in the following materials: LDPE, LLDPE, MDPE, HDPE, PA, EVOH or aluminium.
