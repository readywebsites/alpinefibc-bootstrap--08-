FIBC

Bulk bags, also known as Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC) or jumbo bags, are economical and ideal for storing and transporting powdered, granulated, or bulk products.

FIBC bags are made from UV-stabilized polypropylene woven fabrics. They can carry 500kg to 2000kg loads with safety ratios of 5:1 (single use) or 6:1 (multi-use or UN bags). An optional LDPE liner provides moisture protection.
