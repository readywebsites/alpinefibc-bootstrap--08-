FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Container) also known as Jumbo Bags, Bulk Bags or Big Bags are made of PP woven fabric. It comes in U-panel, Circular and Baffle variety. Depending on usage, you can choose coating, liner, printing, lamination and weight etc. The bags can be customized depending on the customer's need and requirement.

The FIBC bags are used to store and transport dry products in bulk quantity. The bags are highly cost-effective, durable and require very little space to store and transport when empty. It is an ideal packaging solution in industries like agriculture, food, pharma, chemical, cement, mining, construction, plastic, etc.

They are also categorized into the following UN categories for transportation of dangerous goods:

  • ✔ Type A – No electrostatic protection
  • ✔ Type B – Prevents sparks from propagating
  • ✔ Type C – Conductive type, needs to be grounded
  • ✔ Type D – Antistatic protection without grounding
